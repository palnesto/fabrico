HOW TO USE

STEP 1

Hang your clothes on a hanger.

STEP 2

Spray on the shirt evenly.

STEP 3

Wipe the wrinkles off with your hand. In case of hard wrinkles, stretch the fabric side ways and top bottom by holding it at the corners.

STEP 4

Allow it to dry for 4-5 min.
